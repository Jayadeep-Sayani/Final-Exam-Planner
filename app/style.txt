Create a minimalist geometric dashboard website UI.
Use a white background, black structural borders, and orange accent color (#FF6A00).
All shapes must have sharp corners with no border radius.
Use a strict grid layout with evenly spaced rectangular cards.
Typography should be bold geometric sans-serif such as Space Grotesk.
Headings uppercase with tight letter spacing.
Buttons are rectangular with 2px borders and orange hover states.
Icons are simple line icons with 2px stroke width.
No shadows, gradients, or rounded elements.
Layout includes a navigation bar, hero section, stats cards, feature grid, activity list, and system status panels.



Core Design Philosophy

Minimalist, geometric, grid-based interface.

No rounded edges.
No gradients.
No shadows.
Only sharp rectangular shapes.

White background.
Black structure.
Orange accent.

Everything aligns to a rigid grid system.
